About Chama Cha Mapinduzi

Chama Cha Mapinduzi (CCM) is the ruling political party in Tanzania. It has been in power since the country gained independence in 1961.

CCM is known for its commitment to socialism, self-reliance, and the development of Tanzania. Over the years, it has played a significant role in shaping the political landscape and policies of the country.

Membership

Becoming a member of Chama Cha Mapinduzi (CCM) is open to all Tanzanian citizens who share the party's values and goals. To join, interested individuals can visit their local CCM office or apply online through the party's official website.
